About Kristen

From survival
to a stage that changes lives.

Kristen Harootunian's childhood was marked by significant trauma. When she was in 6th grade, her mother — who had struggled with addiction — attempted and completed suicide. Left to navigate her grief alone, Kristen buried her pain and built walls around herself.

As she grew older, she turned to alcohol, drugs, and self-harm to cope. At one point, she believed she wouldn't live past 18. Through numerous treatment efforts, she finally accepted help — attending long-term residential treatment and then a recovery high school to finish her education.

Kristen went on to earn her Bachelor of Arts in Corporate Communications from Penn State University — and was honored to deliver the commencement address at her own graduation.

Since 2016, she has shared her story alongside Minding Your Mind, completing 700+ speaking engagements that have reached over 100,000 individuals across schools, colleges, corporations, hospitals, prisons, and conferences nationwide. She is now more than 10 years sober.

Kristen currently serves as Education Alliance Specialist at La Salle University through Caron Treatment Centers, delivering mental wellness and resilience programming to student athletes. She has been featured in the La Salle Collegian, Telegraph Herald, The Lincoln Center's TLC Connections podcast, Agnes Irwin School Magazine, and more.

Speaking Topics

Every presentation is tailored
to your audience.

Available in-person across the East Coast and nationwide, or virtually. Kristen customizes each talk to the specific needs, demographics, and goals of your organization.

❤
Mental Health & Stigma Reduction

Breaking down barriers around mental illness and addiction in schools, workplaces, and communities. Kristen helps audiences understand stigma, recognize it in themselves and others, and take steps to dismantle it.

🌟
Recovery & Resilience

A candid first-person story of addiction, help-seeking, and building a life in recovery — delivered with hope and honesty. One of the most requested presentations across all audience types.

👥
Youth Mental Health & Early Intervention

Equipping young people with the awareness and tools to recognize warning signs and seek support before crisis. Ideal for schools, colleges, and youth-serving organizations.

🛡
Substance Use Prevention

Evidence-informed programming for student-athletes, youth, and campus communities. Draws on Kristen's direct experience and her work at La Salle University through Caron Treatment Centers.

🏢
Workplace Wellness & Mental Health Awareness

Helping organizations build a culture of openness, support, and psychological safety. Empowers employees and leaders to have real conversations about mental health at work.

⚡
Peak Performance & Resilience

Mental wellness, adaptability, stress management, and communication for high-performance teams and athletes. Tailored specifically to athletic departments and competitive environments.
